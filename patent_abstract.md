## MEDIGUARD: AN ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE-DRIVEN PATIENT HEALTH RECORD MANAGEMENT, PRESCRIPTION ANALYSIS, CONSENT GOVERNANCE, AND CLINICAL SAFETY MONITORING SYSTEM

---

### Abstract

A system that helps patients store, manage, and understand their medical records safely using artificial intelligence is disclosed. A patient logs in through a Vault Application (119) on their mobile device, the Patient Mobile Terminal (118), and uploads their doctor's prescription — whether handwritten or printed — to the MediGuard Artificial Intelligence Processing Center (101). The system first improves the clarity of the uploaded image using a Pre-Processing Unit (104), then reads and extracts the text from the prescription using a Vision Optical Character Recognition Engine (106). A Data Structuring Module (107) organises this information — including the doctor's name, patient details, diagnosis, and medicines — into a clean, readable format. A Medicine Information Module (109) then provides the patient with easy-to-read medicine details in both English and Tamil, covering how to take each medicine, its side effects, and precautions. The patient has full control over who can see their records; doctors listed as Authorized Clinicians (117) can only access records with the patient's permission. In a medical emergency, a clinician may request temporary access through the Secure Network (116). The system also checks the patient's medicines and allergies against known conflicts and warns both the patient and the doctor of any safety risks. All records are locked and secured by an Advanced Encryption Standard 256 Encryption Unit (110) and stored in Firebase Cloud Storage (112) and Firestore Database (113). Every action taken on the patient's records — including who viewed them, any permission changes, and emergency access — is saved by an Audit Log Generator (111) in Consent and Audit Logs (114), giving the patient a complete and trustworthy record of all activity.
